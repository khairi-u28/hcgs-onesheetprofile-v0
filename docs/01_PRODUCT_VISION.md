# One Sheet Profile V2

## Product Vision Document

Version: 2.0
Status: Active
Owner: HCGS Digital Transformation Initiative

---

# 1. Executive Summary

One Sheet Profile is not an employee administration system.

One Sheet Profile is a Workforce Intelligence & Talent Analytics Platform designed to help management and human capital functions understand workforce conditions, identify risks, monitor talent readiness, and support development decisions through data-driven insights.

The platform consolidates employee information, development records, training history, and work experience into a single analytical environment that supports workforce investigation from national level down to individual employee level.

---

# 2. Vision Statement

To become the primary workforce intelligence platform for HCGS by transforming fragmented employee data into actionable management insights.

---

# 3. Product Positioning

The platform is positioned as:

* Workforce Intelligence Platform
* Talent Analytics Platform
* Employee Visibility Platform
* Management Decision Support Platform

The platform is NOT positioned as:

* HRIS Replacement
* Payroll System
* Attendance System
* Recruitment System
* LMS Replacement
* Employee Self Service Portal

---

# 4. Core Objectives

The platform must enable management to:

1. Understand workforce conditions nationally.
2. Identify regions, areas, branches, and employees requiring intervention.
3. Monitor workforce performance and talent readiness.
4. Track employee development journeys.
5. Analyze workforce distribution and capability trends.
6. Support talent and succession discussions using factual data.

---

# 8. Product Principles

## Principle 1

Summary → Visualization → Insight → Detail

Every page must provide all four layers.

---

## Principle 2

Analytics First

The platform should not merely display data.

The platform should help users understand data.

---

## Principle 3

Management Actionability

Every major page should answer:

"What should management do next?"

---

## Principle 4

Drilldown Navigation

National
→ Region
→ Area
→ Branch
→ Employee

---

## Principle 5

Single Source of Truth

All workforce analysis must originate from validated imported datasets.

---

# 9. Success Metrics

Success will be measured by:

* Faster workforce investigation
* Reduced manual reporting effort
* Improved management visibility
* Improved talent monitoring capability
* Faster identification of workforce risks
