# Success Criteria

The platform is successful when management can:

1. Identify workforce risks quickly.
2. Investigate organizational performance efficiently.
3. Monitor talent readiness continuously.
4. Track employee development comprehensively.
5. Make workforce decisions using factual data.

# Long-Term Vision

One Sheet Profile evolves into the central Workforce Intelligence Platform for HCGS, serving as the foundation for future talent analytics, workforce planning, development monitoring, and management decision support initiatives.
